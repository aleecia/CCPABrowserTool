People who live in California are protected by a relatively new law, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
Our browser extension can help protect your privacy following the rights defined by CCPA, as briefly shown below.

1. The right to know about the personal information a business collects about you and how it is used and shared;
2. The right to delete personal information collected from you (with some exceptions);
3. The right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information;
