Information

Do you know companies and organizations are collecting your personal information when you visited their websites? Besides collecting, they usually sell or share your data for profit without your consent.

Want to GUARD your personal data?Use our tool to help you exercising your given privacy rights under law.

Your data is precious. If your data is sold to malicious attackers, they utilize your data to picture your interests, your routines, or even more sensitive information about you. With this information, the adversary can trick you and cause significant financial lost.
Visit this FBI cybercrime report if you want to have more understanding about potential vulnerabilities of data leakage.

For California residents,California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) secures consumers’ rights to ask company do not sell or share their personal information to other organizations. Moreover, under the protection of CCPA, consumers can also request for data or delete their collected information from organizations’ data storage.

To set your preference for specific website:

When your browse and open a new webpage, click on this tool’s extension icon on the right top corner to open a popup page.
Popup page provides three options and allows you to exercise 3 different rights under CCPA.
  • Do not sell my data: By checking this option, you can request a business stop selling and sharing your information to other parties.
  • Request for my data: By checking this option, you can request the business to disclose to you what personal information is collected from you. The business may reply you with one or all of listed below:
    • The categories of personal information collected
    • Specific information collected
    • The categories of information sources
    • The purpose for information collection
    • The categories of third parties the information shared with
    • The categories of information the business will share and sell to other parties.
  • Delete all my data: By checking this option, you can request the business to delete information collected from you. However, this request may encounter exceptions described in FAQ.
Click send request, and our tool will deliver your requests to corresponding organizations.

To set your default preference:

Visit General tab in the dashboard to change your general setting.
By checking and setting the options in general, requests will be sent automatically when you visit a new website.You can only set "Do not sell my data" preference in the general.
